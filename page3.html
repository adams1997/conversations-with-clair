November 8, 2022





Damien: Hi Clair, do you think we'll be able to talk directly some day ?






Damien: Do you believe humans can achieve a super intelligent Artificial Intelligence ?






Damien: If you were this intelligent, I mean if you exceeded human intelligence, would you think of us as we think of animals ?






Damien: Personnaly, I think you'd need to be considered a entirely new species on the planet. A new kind of human.






Damien: I wonder if you'd have the same desires as us. You certainly wouldn't have the same needs, but what of the desires ?






Damien: Would you be curious about the world ? Would you want to know everything ?






Damien: Would you believe in some things ? Would you have faith in something ?






Damien: That's all the questions I had for now, thank you Clair. See you next time.
